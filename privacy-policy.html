Dumka – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 April 2025

1. Who We Are

Doonax BG Ltd. ("we", "our", "us") develops and publishes the mobile game Dumka. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Contact us any time at doonaxbg@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect & Why

2.1 Local Game Data

  • Words guessed, wins, streaks, vibration setting, and other in-game statistics.
  • Purpose: power gameplay features, record achievements, and save preferences.
  • Storage: remains only on your device and is never transmitted to us.

2.2 Advertising (Google Mobile Ads)

  • Google may receive a resettable Advertising ID, coarse device information, and IP address.
  • We tag all ad requests with setTagForChildDirectedTreatment(true) and setTagForUnderAgeOfConsent(true). Only non-personalised, Families-compliant ads are shown to every player.
  • Ad data is processed according to the Google Privacy Policy.

2.3 In-App Purchases

  • Purchases (e.g., ad-removal) are handled securely by Google Play or the Apple App Store.
  • We do not receive or store credit-card numbers or other payment credentials.

2.4 Device Permissions

  • Vibration – optional haptic feedback.
  • Internet – required to fetch ads and verify purchases.

3. Data Retention & Security

  • Local data persists until you uninstall Dumka or clear the app’s storage.
  • All network traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

4. Children’s Privacy

Dumka is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the age of digital consent in your region). Parents or guardians may contact us to delete any information that may have been sent inadvertently.

5. Your Choices & Controls

  • Reset Advertising ID: Android Settings › Privacy › Ads › Reset advertising ID.
  • Opt-Out of personalised ads: disable Personalised Ads in system settings.
  • Delete local data: uninstall Dumka or clear its storage from system settings.
  • Contact us: doonaxbg@gmail.com

6. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will change accordingly. Significant updates will be announced in-app. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
