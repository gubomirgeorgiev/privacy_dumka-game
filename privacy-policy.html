Dumka – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 August 2025

1. Who We Are

Doonax BG (“we”, “our”, “us”) is the trading name of Lyubomir Georgiev, an independent developer based in Bulgaria. Contact: doonaxbg@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect & Why

2.1 Local Game Data

  • Words guessed, wins, streaks, haptics and other in-game stats.
  • Purpose: provide gameplay features, achievements and preferences.
  • Storage: only on your device; not transmitted to us.

2.2 Advertising (Google AdMob)

  • We use Google’s Families Self-Certified Ads SDK (AdMob) to show non-personalized, G-rated ads only.
  • All ad requests are tagged tagForChildDirectedTreatment(true) and tagForUnderAgeOfConsent(true).
  • Google may receive a resettable Advertising ID, coarse device info and IP address to deliver, measure and protect against fraud.
  • Ad processing is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

2.3 Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics & Performance)

  • These services collect app events, crash traces and performance metrics only if you opt in inside the app (EEA/UK users are asked on first launch and can change their choice at any time).
  • Purpose: understand app usage, fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Data is processed by Google Firebase under their product-specific terms and privacy policy.

2.4 In-App Purchases

  • Purchases (e.g., ad removal) are handled by Google Play / Apple App Store. We do not receive payment card data.

2.5 Device Permissions

  • Vibration (haptics) and Internet (ads, updates, purchase verification).

3. Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Consent: analytics, crash reporting and performance monitoring (you can withdraw anytime in the app).
  • Contract / legitimate interests: provide the game and show non-personalized, contextual ads to fund the free service.

4. Children’s Privacy & Families Policy

Dumka is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are non-personalized, age-appropriate and requested via Google’s UMP. Parents/guardians may contact us to remove any information sent inadvertently.

5. Your Choices & Controls

  • Manage analytics/crash/performance: open Settings → Privacy in the app to enable/disable.
  • Reset Advertising ID: Android Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID.
  • Delete local data: uninstall the app or clear its storage.
  • Contact us: doonaxbg@gmail.com

6. Data Security & Transfers

  • All traffic uses HTTPS/TLS. We use Firebase App Check to help protect our backend resources from abuse.
  • Google may process data on servers outside your country; transfers are covered by their data protection terms.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Significant changes will be announced in-app. Continued use after an update constitutes acceptance.
